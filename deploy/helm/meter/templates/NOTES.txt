Reach the engine and control plane from your cluster:

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "meter.fullname" . }}-engine {{ .Values.engine.listenPort }}:{{ .Values.engine.listenPort }}
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "meter.fullname" . }}-control-plane {{ .Values.controlPlane.port }}:{{ .Values.controlPlane.port }}

Then:

  curl http://localhost:{{ .Values.engine.listenPort }}/health
  curl http://localhost:{{ .Values.controlPlane.port }}/health

For production, supply real images (engine.image / controlPlane.image), point at managed Postgres and
ClickHouse (set postgres.enabled=false / clickhouse.enabled=false and the *.databaseUrl / clickhouseUrl),
and scale the stateless engine with engine.replicas.
